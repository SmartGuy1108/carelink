CareLink – Voice-first AI companion

One large button → Tap and speak.

AI responds in Hindi, English, or Hinglish.

Handles daily conversations and reminders.

How It Works

  1. Parent taps the mic and speaks naturally
  2. AI understands the request and responds by voice
  3. AI classifies the request: chat, task, or emergency
  4. Family group receives a notification with context

Core Features

  • ✅ Emergency Escalation
